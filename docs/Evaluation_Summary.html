AgroGuard Shield – Evaluation Brief (Smart India Hackathon 2025)

Department: Computer Science and Engineering
Jury: _____________________  |   Team/SIH Batch No: _____________________

1) Novelty of the Idea

  • End‑to‑end biosecurity platform for pig/poultry farms unifying risk assessments, compliance, alerts and training.
  • Multi‑stakeholder dashboards: Farmer, Veterinarian, Extension Worker, Regulator, Researcher.
  • India‑first localization: 10+ regional languages (incl. Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi).
  • Actionable intelligence with alerts, compliance snapshots and farm‑type specific training.

2) Complexity / Clarity / Scale of Impact

  • Stack: React + TypeScript + Vite + Tailwind; Supabase (Postgres + RLS) for auth/data; React Query.
  • Security: RBAC in app + RLS in DB for least‑privilege, role‑scoped visibility.
  • Features: assessments, compliance, Alert Center (filters/read/unread/CSV), training (video/text, progress, search/filter/sort).
  • Scales from single farms to district/state programmes; exportable reports.

3) Feasibility / Practicability

  • Deployable on low‑cost infra (Supabase + static hosting).
  • Demo SQL data with idempotent inserts; validated UUIDs/foreign keys.
  • Mobile‑first, low‑bandwidth friendly; CSV exports for offline workflows.

4) User Experience / Sustainability

  • Design tokens, persistent dark mode, accessible contrast and keyboard operability.
  • Micro‑interactions: skeletons, animated cards, progress rings.
  • Training with progress + completion, video or text content; farmer‑first localization.
  • Maintainable codebase: typed components, reusable charts, CSV utility, saved‑views hook; comprehensive docs.

5) Potential for Future Work / Progression

  • PWA/offline sync; push notifications for high‑severity alerts.
  • Global drill‑downs and saved views; quizzes/certifications in training.
  • Integrations: IoT sensors, lab results, government APIs; enterprise white‑label.
  • Governance: audit logs, advanced RLS, error monitoring.

6) Overall – Why This Solution Matters

  • Unifies field operations (farmer), clinical oversight (vet), training (extension) and policy (regulator).
  • Practical, affordable stack with real demo data; runs on low‑end devices.
  • Reduces outbreak risk, improves compliance and strengthens data‑driven policymaking.

Demo Highlights (What to show)

  • Landing: tokens, dark mode, testimonials, pricing and FAQ.
  • Farmer: farms, assessments, compliance tracker, training (search/filter/sort), alerts.
  • Veterinarian: client farms, reports, analytics, activity feed.
  • Extension: modules, training admin, progress tracking.
  • Regulator: surveillance placeholders, Alert Center (filters/read/unread/CSV), analytics.
  • i18n: language switch across farmer flows.

Suggested Scores

  • Complexity/Impact: 9/10
  • Feasibility: 8/10
  • UX/Sustainability: 9/10
  • Future Work: 9/10
